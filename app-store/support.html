Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a medication?

Open Settings (or the first setup screen), tap “Add medication,” enter the name and optional dose, tap the time field to pick a time, then Save.

How do I confirm I've taken a dose?

On the Today screen, the dose that's due shows a “Confirm Dose” button. Tap it once you've taken your medicine. Your progress ring and streak update right away.

How do caregiver alerts work?

In Settings, add a primary caregiver and (optionally) a secondary contact, and turn on the “Email” reminder. If a dose runs late, your primary caregiver is emailed; if it's missed, your secondary contact is added too.

Will I get reminders?

Yes — enable Notifications (and optionally Alarm sound) in Settings. Reminders fire at each scheduled dose time. Make sure notifications are allowed for VitaCare in your phone settings.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your medications and history are visible only to you, encrypted in transit and on your device. We don't show ads or sell data. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, medications, and history. You can also email us to request deletion.

I forgot my password.

VitaCare uses a username and password. If you're locked out, contact us at the email above and we'll help you recover access.
