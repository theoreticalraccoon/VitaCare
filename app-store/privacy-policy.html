VitaCare Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

VitaCare (“we”, “us”) is operated by Team Rajadhe, based in Sri Lanka. This policy explains what information the VitaCare mobile app collects, how we use it, and your choices. By using VitaCare you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect what the app needs to function. We do not use advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking.

  • Account information: your display name and the username you choose (used to create your login). Your password is handled by our authentication provider and stored only as a secure cryptographic hash — we never see or store it in plain text.
  • Health information: the medications you add (name, dose, time) and your dose history (taken, late, or missed, with timestamps).
  • Care circle contacts: the names and email addresses of any caregiver or family members you choose to add so they can receive dose alerts.
  • Preferences: your reminder settings.

We do not collect location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or device advertising identifiers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide the core service: scheduling, reminders, dose confirmation, and history.
  • To send caregiver alerts by email when a dose is late or missed — only if you enable the Email reminder option and provide a caregiver email.
  • To authenticate you and keep your data synced across your devices.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

3. Service Providers

  • Supabase — hosts our database and authentication. Your data is stored there and protected by access controls so that only your account can read or write it.
  • Resend — delivers caregiver alert emails on our behalf (receives the caregiver's email address and the relevant dose details).

4. How We Protect Your Data

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). On your device, session tokens and cached health data are encrypted at rest using your device's secure keystore. In our database, row-level security restricts every record to its owner.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from within the app (Settings → Delete account), which permanently removes your profile, medications, and dose history. You may also email us at shavirukasthuriarachchi@gmail.com to request deletion.

6. Your Rights

You may access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, use the in-app controls or contact us.

7. Children's Privacy

VitaCare is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction) and we do not knowingly collect their data. If a medication schedule is managed on behalf of a minor, it should be done by a parent, guardian, or caregiver.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
