## App Store Description (4000 char max)
```
VitaCare helps you take the right medication at the right time — and keeps the people who care about you in the loop.

Unlike plain reminder apps that only buzz and hope, VitaCare adds real accountability: you confirm each dose, and if one runs late or gets missed, your caregivers are notified automatically. It's built to be calm, clear, and easy to use, even for those who aren't comfortable with technology.

WHY VITACARE
Around half of people with long-term conditions don't take medication as prescribed. The usual culprit isn't willpower — it's that a notification is easy to ignore and nobody knows when a dose is skipped. VitaCare closes that gap with confirmation and a caregiver care circle.

KEY FEATURES
- SIMPLE MEDICATION SCHEDULE — Add each medicine with its dose and time in seconds.
- ONE-TAP DOSE CONFIRMATION — Confirm a dose the moment you take it. Your daily progress ring fills as you go.
- CARE CIRCLE ACCOUNTABILITY — Add a primary caregiver and a secondary family contact. If a dose is late, your primary caregiver is alerted; if it's missed, your secondary contact is added too.
- ADHERENCE STREAKS & HISTORY — See your streak, adherence rate, and a clear timeline of every dose.
- FLEXIBLE REMINDERS — Choose push notifications, an alarm sound, and email summaries.
- CLEAN, ACCESSIBLE DESIGN — Large touch targets, big readable text, and color-coded doses (green = taken, amber = due, red = missed).

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Your data is yours. Medications and history are protected so only you can see them, encrypted in transit and on your device. No ads. No trackers. No selling your data.

GREAT FOR
- Anyone managing daily or chronic medication
- Older adults and the families who help care for them
- Caregivers who want peace of mind without hovering

Set up your schedule, add your care circle, and never wonder whether today's doses were taken.

Account required (free). VitaCare is a personal organization tool and does not provide medical advice; always follow your doctor's and pharmacist's instructions.
```

HOW TO TEST:

2. On first run you'll add medications: enter a name, optional dose, tap the time field to pick a time, then "Add to schedule." Add at least one.
3. Step 2 of setup is the "care circle" (optional caregiver emails) — you can skip it.
4. On the Today screen, tap "Confirm Dose" to confirm the due medication. The progress ring and streak update immediately.
5. The History tab shows adherence stats and a timeline. Settings lets you edit meds, reminders, caregivers, and sign out.

NOTES FOR REVIEW:
- No special hardware needed. Works offline; data syncs to our backend (Supabase) when online.
- Caregiver EMAIL alerts are only sent if the "Email" reminder toggle is on AND a caregiver email is set; they are delivered via our backend (Resend). They are not required to test the app.
- Push notifications/alarms fire at scheduled dose times on a real device.
